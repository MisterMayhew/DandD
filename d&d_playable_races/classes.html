Good Morning

With the D&D playable race chosen we will lightly touch base on the classes that you adventure with, rounding out your character to their fullest potential.

And in case I don't see you

Much like your D&D playable race you will have to ask yourself and the Dungeon Master and quick series of questions:

  • What will your Dungeon Master, or DM, allow?
  • What has the rest of your adventuring party chosen?
  • What would your dream job be?
  • What makes you happy?

Trying to do our best living the realm of possibilities we are going to try and give an accurate and vague mention to the playable classes. This way if you have decided to dream big and become an astronaut, we might be able to give a class that could help with this dream. And say if there is someone out there who has the dream to become a UFC fighter, I feel that we can make some connection here for you.

Playable Classes

Now that you have consulted with your party and Dungeon Master, it is time to select a D&D Playable Class that is tailored to your imagination and aspirations.

----- Close your eyes and search within -----

Now Choose

Barbarian

  • Pronunciation - Bar-bair-e-en
  • With little care of collateral damage they go into full rage mode and destroy the largest foes
  • Standing for the clan above all else they are horrible people on long road trips
  • Your Adventuring Job
    • Personal Trainer
    • Exotic Chef
    • Private Security

Bard

  • Pronunciation - Bard
  • For those gifted with song and tales, your charisma will carry you through life
  • A supportive person in a party they get the party pumped up and degrade the enemy
  • Your Adventuring Job
    • Musician
    • Comedian
    • Motivational Speaker

Cleric

  • Pronunciation - Kl-air-ic
  • With divine powers coming from a deity and power in faith, these adventurers bring all the heals
  • Do not let that fool you though ... with great power comes great responsibility
  • I have known many clerics fall to the dark side and become a guardian of their deity and heal no one
  • Your Adventuring Job
    • Priest
    • Doctor
    • Grave Robber

Druid

  • Pronunciation - Droo-id
  • Having the power to bend mother nature's will at your finger tips
  • Conjure or control animals and get them to do thy bidding
  • Heal the earth from the ravages of time or the collateral damage from the barbarians
  • Your Adventuring Job
    • Animal Whisperer
    • Veterinarian
    • Landscape Architect

Fighter

  • Pronunciation - Fi-tr
  • A warrior with the skills to command a battle or take on large foes
  • Your Adventuring Job
    • UFC Fighter
    • Bouncer
    • Military

Monk

  • Pronunciation - M-un-k
  • Personal faith in lining up all chakras have made you a devastating unarmed weapon
  • Being in close combat attacking with bare fists is a dangerous path to walk
  • Try not to get in over your head against the metal paladin knights ... just think of your hands
  • Your Adventuring Job
    • Advertising Salesman
    • Self Defense Teacher
    • Mime

Paladin

  • Pronunciation - Pal-a-din
  • These are the crusaders that come in hard and fast to save their fellow adventurers
  • Usually armored in heavy plated garb they attack in the name of their deity
  • Your Adventuring Job
    • Demolition Artist
    • Emergency Responder
    • Armored Priest

Ranger

  • Pronunciation - Rain-jer
  • Quick and intelligent they use their surroundings as cover and take out foes from a distance.
  • For those who have seen Lord of the Rings, Legolas.
  • For those who have not let us try this one, Robin Hood.
  • Your Adventuring Job
    • Sniper
    • Scout
    • Rock Climbing Instructor

Rouge

  • Pronunciation - Row-ge
  • These sneaky devils exist in the shadows and only come out for loot
  • Depending on your path you can infiltrate for the good or evil of the world
  • Your Adventuring Job
    • Thief
    • Assassin
    • Information Broker

Sorcerer

  • Pronunciation - Sore-sir-er
  • Born into the world with the gift of magic you spend less time learning and more time practicing
  • The elements of this world have nothing on you as you control them all without effort
  • Where the wizards have to learn and build magic it comes naturally to you
  • Your Adventuring Job
    • Pyrotechnician
    • Welder
    • Neighborhood Watch

Warlock

  • Pronunciation - War-lock
  • Looking for more power then others you have made a deal with a devil that now supplies you with the power you desired
  • Having made a deal they can call on you to do their bidding no matter the casualties
  • Some how you and clerics will not see eye to eye on many topics
  • Your Adventuring Job
    • CEO of large company
    • Engineer of Walmart parking lots
    • Fiddler

Wizard

  • Pronunciation - Wiz-urd
  • Having to learn the ability to bend and shape the world to their whims
  • They use their high intelligence to use and learn more spells then anyone but a the cost of needing to prepare the spells before hand
  • Your Adventuring Job
    • Teacher
    • Librarian
    • Magician
